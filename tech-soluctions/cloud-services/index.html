Cloud Services

  • Cloud Service

• Migration Assessment: assess existing applications, infrastructure, and workloads to determine the feasibility and readiness for migration to the cloud. • Strategy and Planning: help businesses develop a comprehensive cloud migration strategy, including selecting the appropriate cloud platform and determining the most suitable migration approach (lift and shift, re-platforming, or refactoring). • Application and Data Migration: assist in migrating applications and data from on-premises environments or other cloud platforms to the target cloud infrastructure. This may involve reconfiguring applications, optimizing code, or implementing data replication. • Cloud Infrastructure Deployment: set up the necessary cloud infrastructure, including virtual machines, storage, networking, and security measures required for running applications in the cloud. • Optimize and Refine: conduct testing and optimization to ensure applications perform efficiently in the cloud environment. They may also help with ongoing monitoring and management of the cloud resources. • Training and Support: offer training programs to help businesses understand the new cloud environment, as well as ongoing technical support and guidance to address any issues that may arise during or after migration. • Cost Optimization: assist in optimizing cloud costs by recommending the use of reserved instances, right-sizing resources, and implementing auto-scaling to align usage with business demands. • Security and Compliance: ensure that the migrated applications and data follow best security practices and comply with relevant regulations. They may implement security controls, encryption, and access management to protect data in the cloud.
