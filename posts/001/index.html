AI-Powered Privileged Access Management

  • AI
  • Security

The first-ever Teccelerates physical event, held in collaboration with Delinea was successfully concluded on Aug 30! Together, we delved deep into the realm of AI-Powered Privileged Access Management, and here’s a wrap.

The future of Security: Albert Chu, Sales Engineer, APAC, Delinea, inspired us with his expertise in Privileged Access Management. The insights shed light on how to build robust defences against cyber threats, ensuring our digital landscapes remain secure.

AI – reshaping our future: Dicky Fung, Solution Consultant, Teccelerates, took us on a journey through the boundless potential of Artificial Intelligence. InSoruce, the latest solution from Teccelerates, demonstrated how AI is reshaping industries, enhancing customer experiences, and reshaping the way we work and play with natural language processing.

AI and Security: The live demonstration of AI-Powered Privileged Access Management was a highlight. Witnessing AI algorithms enhance security protocols showcased the potential of technology in safeguarding our digital assets.

To our incredible speakers, Erica Man, Elton Cheong, Albert Chu and Dicky Fung, thank you for making this event a resounding success. Special thanks to Priscilla Goh and the marketing team, without them, this event won’t be able to run so smoothly. Last but not least, our attendees, your passion for innovation and security made this event unforgettable.

Let’s continue the conversation and keep the momentum going! See you all in next event!
